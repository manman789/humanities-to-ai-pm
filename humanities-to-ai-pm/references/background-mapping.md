# Background Mapping

Use this reference to translate humanities and social science backgrounds into credible AI product manager competencies.

## Chinese, Linguistics, Foreign Languages

Transferable strengths:

- Natural language understanding: semantic nuance, intent, discourse, pragmatics, ambiguity.
- Text quality evaluation: fluency, coherence, tone, style, factual consistency.
- Corpus thinking: examples, annotation rules, category definitions, edge cases.
- Prompt expression: instruction clarity, role framing, output constraints, evaluation criteria.
- Conversational experience: user intent, turn-taking, clarification, error recovery.

Suitable AI PM directions:

- AI writing tools, writing assistants, proofreading, rewriting, and style transfer.
- Chatbots, intelligent customer service, conversational learning companions.
- NLP products, annotation platforms, evaluation systems, prompt tools.
- Knowledge base Q&A, RAG, content intelligence, document processing.

Resume translation examples:

- "语言学研究" -> "抽象语言现象与用户表达模式，能够拆解自然语言任务中的意图、语义边界和输出质量标准。"
- "语料分析" -> "具备语料分类、标签体系设计和文本质量评估意识，可支持 AI 产品的样本构建与效果评估。"
- "文本写作/修改" -> "能够定义多场景文本生成需求，设计输出规范、风格约束和质量验收标准。"

## Education, Normal University, Teaching

Transferable strengths:

- User分层: learners by level, goal, motivation, difficulty, and feedback need.
- Learning path design: objectives, scaffolding, practice, feedback, assessment.
- Scenario understanding: teacher preparation, classroom use, homework correction, self-study.
- Content product design: curriculum structure, knowledge points, exercises, rubrics.
- Human-in-the-loop: teacher review, parent feedback, student safety, evaluation.

Suitable AI PM directions:

- AI tutoring, intelligent homework correction, teacher assistants, lesson planning.
- Learning companion bots, personalized learning paths, educational content generation.
- K12, language learning, vocational learning, enterprise training.

Resume translation examples:

- "教学设计" -> "围绕学习目标拆解用户路径，设计内容结构、练习机制和反馈闭环。"
- "课堂观察" -> "通过真实教学场景识别用户痛点，沉淀学习者分层、任务流程和产品机会。"

## Journalism, Communication, Editing, Publishing

Transferable strengths:

- Content strategy, information hierarchy, topic planning, editing standards.
- User attention, distribution channels, content growth, tone control.
- AIGC workflow design: generation, review, compliance, distribution, iteration.

Suitable AI PM directions:

- AIGC content platforms, content operations tools, media productivity tools.
- AI copywriting, content review, SEO writing, knowledge management.

## Psychology, Sociology, Anthropology

Transferable strengths:

- User research, interview design, qualitative coding, behavioral insight.
- Motivation, decision-making, group behavior, identity and trust.
- Scenario modeling and ethical sensitivity.

Suitable AI PM directions:

- User research-heavy AI products, mental health companion products with caution, community tools, service automation.

## Literature, History, Philosophy

Transferable strengths:

- Complex text understanding, knowledge organization, argument structure.
- Critical thinking, judgment, value-sensitive product thinking.
- Long-form content, narrative design, expert knowledge products.

Suitable AI PM directions:

- Knowledge products, research assistants, reading tools, cultural content AI, document intelligence.

## Positioning Rule

Use "I understand language/content/users/scenarios deeply, and I am learning AI product methods to turn that understanding into usable products." Avoid "I know AI algorithms" unless evidence exists.
