# AI PM Competency

Use this model to evaluate or explain AI product manager readiness.

## Core Competencies

1. User and scenario insight
   - Identify target users, pain points, jobs-to-be-done, constraints, and success conditions.

2. Product definition
   - Define MVP scope, user stories, interaction flow, acceptance criteria, and iteration priorities.

3. AI capability understanding
   - Understand what LLMs, RAG, agents, prompts, embeddings, and evaluation can and cannot do at application level.

4. Data and evaluation thinking
   - Define input data, examples, labels, quality criteria, metrics, and feedback loops.

5. Interaction and experience design
   - Design conversational flows, clarification, fallback, user control, transparency, and trust.

6. Risk and safety awareness
   - Consider hallucination, bias, privacy, misuse, content safety, and human review.

7. Cross-functional communication
   - Communicate with engineers, designers, operations, content experts, sales, and domain stakeholders.

8. Business and growth judgment
   - Understand customer value, use frequency, willingness to pay, competitive differentiation, and adoption friction.

## AI PM Knowledge Checklist

- LLM basics: tokens, context window, temperature, prompts, limitations.
- Prompt engineering: role, task, context, constraints, examples, evaluation.
- RAG: chunking, embedding, retrieval, reranking, generation, citation, evaluation.
- Agent basics: tool use, planning, memory, workflow orchestration, failure modes.
- AI evaluation: accuracy, relevance, faithfulness, helpfulness, latency, cost, user satisfaction.
- Product metrics: activation, retention, task success rate, completion time, deflection rate, conversion.

## Candidate Readiness Levels

Entry-ready:

- Can analyze AI product scenarios, write clear PRDs, design prompts, compare competitors, and explain risks.

Stronger:

- Has a portfolio project with user problem, prototype or demo, evaluation plan, and iteration logic.

Most credible:

- Has shipped, tested, or operated an AI feature with user feedback or measurable outcomes.
