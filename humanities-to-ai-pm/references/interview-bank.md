# Interview Bank

Use this reference to prepare AI product manager interview answers.

## Transition Questions

### Why AI product manager?

Answer structure:

1. Direct answer: AI PM connects user problems, language/content understanding, and product implementation.
2. Background bridge: humanities training helps with user expression, semantic nuance, content quality, and scenario understanding.
3. Evidence: mention a project, JD analysis, PRD, prototype, prompt experiment, or AI tool study.
4. Growth plan: product methods, AI basics, data/evaluation, technical collaboration.

### Why can a humanities background do AI PM?

Key points:

- AI application products often fail not only because of models, but because scenarios, instructions, evaluation, and user trust are poorly designed.
- Humanities backgrounds can contribute to language quality, content standards, user research, and knowledge organization.
- The candidate must补强 product methods, AI concepts, and measurable project evidence.

## Product Design Questions

### Design an AI writing assistant.

Cover:

- Target user and scenario.
- Writing task type.
- Input and output.
- Rubric.
- Feedback flow.
- Rewrite interaction.
- Evaluation metrics.
- Hallucination and over-correction risks.

### Design an AI education product.

Cover:

- Learner/teacher/user split.
- Learning goal.
- Content and feedback loop.
- Personalization.
- Teacher or parent review.
- Safety and age appropriateness.
- Learning outcome metrics.

### Design a RAG knowledge base.

Cover:

- Document source and permission.
- Chunking and metadata.
- Retrieval and reranking.
- Answer with citation.
- Unknown-answer handling.
- Evaluation set.
- Feedback loop.

## AI PM Knowledge Questions

### How do you evaluate an AI product?

Mention:

- Task success rate.
- Answer accuracy/relevance.
- Faithfulness to source.
- User satisfaction.
- Completion time.
- Retention or repeat use.
- Cost and latency.
- Manual review and sample audit.

### What if the model output is unstable?

Mention:

- Clarify task scope and input format.
- Improve prompt and examples.
- Add retrieval/context.
- Add output schema and validation.
- Add confidence, fallback, and human review.
- Build evaluation set and regression tests.

## Answer Style

Use confident but grounded phrasing. Do not sound like the candidate is apologizing for a humanities background. Do not pretend the candidate is already an algorithm expert.
